Run Analysis

Tatum draws on her 20 years experience at the athletics track, and the knowledge she has gathered from some of the best physiotherapists and analysts from around the world to give you straight-to-the-point advice about running technique, shoes and biomechanics

Tatum is a REPS Level 3 Personal Trainer, who works with clients in the Sevenoaks, Tonbridge & Tunbridge Wells areas in Kent, and has the equipment required to travel to the client to provide the ultimate in convenient and personalised training
