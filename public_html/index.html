TAKE YOUR FOOTBALL TO THE NEXT LEVEL


Speedwork and sprint training are essential components of football. In the ever increasingly competitive world of sport, the right speed training and sprint technique will give young footballers the edge, and take their game to the next level.

Through my “7 Point Speed Training Programme" I incorporate all of the speed training footballers need from sprint technique and focussed specific speed work to acceleration, change of direction, plyometrics, and everything in between.

I will analyse sprint technique, make any necessary adaptations, apply my speed programme and I will improve the speed of any footballer.

In addition to speed and sprint work I include specific training designed to build and maintain the fitness base required for football. This includes football focussed conditioning work, circuit training and injury prevention which are all key to longevity and continuity of football training.

Why Speed Training With Me?


Drawing on my background as an international GB sprinter, when I work with footballers, I apply the sprint techniques and speed training that I have learned covering every element of speedwork needed to improve the performance in their game.

I started sprinting at the age of 11, competed for England by the age of 13 and was one of the fastest girls in the world by the age of 14. I have trained and competed at the highest level of sprinting being fortunate enough to travel the world gaining invaluable and incomparable knowledge. I have trained alongside some of the greatest coaches and athletes in the world and I now place all of this knowledge into football speed training with me.
